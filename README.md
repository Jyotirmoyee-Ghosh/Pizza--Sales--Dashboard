1. Introduction
The Pizza Sales Dashboard Project is created to analyze and visualize pizza sales data in an interactive and user-friendly manner. The dashboard helps in understanding sales performance, customer ordering patterns, and revenue trends.

This project uses visual tools such as bar charts, donut charts, and slicers to make data analysis easy and effective.
The dashboard includes a Year slicer for 2022 and 2023, allowing users to compare sales performance between these two years.

2. Objectives of the Project
The main objectives of this project are:
To analyze pizza sales data for the years 2022 and 2023
To identify top-selling pizzas
To understand sales by category and size
To analyze monthly and weekday sales trends
To create an interactive dashboard using slicers
To help management take better business decisions.

3. Dataset Description
The dataset contains pizza sales information with the following key details:
Order ID
Order Date
Pizza Name
Pizza Category
Pizza Size
Quantity Sold
Total Price
The data is cleaned and formatted before creating the dashboard to ensure accuracy.

4. Tools Used
Microsoft Excel / WPS Excel
Pivot Tables
Pivot Charts
Slicers
Donut Charts
Bar Charts

5. Dashboard Features
5.1 KPI Cards
The dashboard displays important Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):
Total Orders
Total Revenue
Total Pizzas Sold
Average Orders
These KPIs give a quick overview of business performance.

5.2 Year Slicer (2022–2023)
A Year slicer is provided for 2022 and 2023
Users can select:
Only 2022
Only 2023
Both years together
All charts and KPIs update automatically based on the selected year.

5.3 Pizza Sales by Category
Shows sales distribution across categories such as:
Classic
Supreme
Veggie
Chicken
Helps identify which category generates the highest sales

5.4 Pizza Sales by Size
Visualizes sales by pizza size:
Small
Medium
Large
Extra Large
Medium and Large pizzas show higher demand compared to others.

5.5 Top 5 Most Ordered Pizzas
Displays the top 5 pizzas based on total orders
Helps in identifying customer preferences.

5.6 Total Orders Trend by Month
Shows monthly sales trends
Helps identify peak sales months and low-performing months.

Useful for planning promotions and offers
5.7 Total Orders by Weekdays
Displays sales distribution across weekdays
Helps understand customer behavior on different days

5.8 Top 5 Most Selling Pizzas (%)
Shows percentage contribution of top pizzas
Highlights best-performing products.

6. Key Insights
Sales performance varies between 2022 and 2023
Certain pizza categories contribute more to revenue
Medium and Large size pizzas are most preferred
Specific months and weekdays show higher sales
Top pizzas contribute a major share of total revenue.

7. Conclusion
The Pizza Sales Dashboard provides a clear and interactive way to analyze sales data for the years 2022 and 2023.
By using slicers, charts, and KPIs, the dashboard helps in identifying trends, understanding customer preferences, and improving business decisions.
This project demonstrates the effective use of data visualization and analysis techniques for real-world business problems.
